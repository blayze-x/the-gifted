# Characters — The Gifted

All 8 playable characters are original anime-inspired designs.
Every character has identical stats — the choice is purely cosmetic.

## Shared Abilities

All characters share the same abilities regardless of selected style:

- **Move** — Walk left/right (speed: 2.2)
- **Jump** — Standard jump height (-11.5 velocity)
- **Dash** — Short burst of speed (7.0 velocity, 110 frame cooldown)
- **Magic Blast** — Fires a projectile in facing direction (no cooldown)

---

## Visual Anatomy

Each character is drawn with:
- Hair (color + highlight)
- Head / face
- Two glowing eyes (color varies per character)
- Mouth
- Ear
- Cape (two-tone, animated wave)
- Body / torso with shirt stripe
- Belt + buckle
- Two arms (front arm extends when shooting)
- Legs with walking animation
- Boots
- Optional extras: horns, ears, tail, wings, mask, armor, lightning, void ring, rune glyph
- Chest aura glow (character color)
- Ground shadow ellipse
